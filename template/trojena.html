14.11.2023
Technical evaluation process for Trojena dams has been completed.
Trojena is negotiating the commercial aspects of the dam structures used to create the central lake at Trojena and will select one contractor from Saudi Arabia-based Al-Ayuni, Turkiye-headquartered Limak, Beijing-based PowerChina and Italy’s WeBuild. The contract is expected to last 26-month contract and be valued at approximatey 2 billion USD.
13.11.2023
Trojena developing infrastructure for sustainable snow production.
Approximately 75 percent of the slopes will be covered with machine-made snow, created in the “most sustainable way possible by using only water and air as ingredients. No chemical additives will be used in Trojena’s snow production. In terms of skiing, all energy used will be from renewable sources, with the water coming from zero discharge desalination plants. melted water from the snow production will be recycled.

Trojena's 'The Vault' tenders are out and construction has started.
Workers have started construction on the gateway to Trojena vertical village - The Vault, constructed within the mountain with a fusion of technology, residential, entertainment and hospitality facilities.
25.09.2023
Zaha Hadid Architects designs crystalline tower for Trojena.
Zaha Hadid Architects has planned the symbol of Trojena, a crystalline, 330-meter-tall skyscraper overlooking the artificial lake to accommodate retail outlets, restaurants, exhibition spaces, and observation decks at varying heights, connected by cable car to the lakeside development. Trojena will offer skiing from December to March on 36 kilometers of ski slopes as well as an arena for adventure sports.
09.08.2023
Minor Hotels to open an Anantara branded property in Trojena.
The 270-key Anantara resort, in the Water Village on the man-made lake in Trojena, will offer guest rooms and suites with lake or mountain views, two specialty restaurants, the Anantara Spa and infinity pool with mountain and lake views, outdoor cinema, a kids’ clubs. Guests can experience camel and horse riding, and guided walks around the area.

The W Hotel in Trojena is anticipated to feature 236 guest rooms, including 47 suites, with views of the mountains and a freshwater lake. The JW Marriott Hotel in Trojena will be a haven for luxury travelers who seek an ultra-premium escape paired with exceptional service and well-being experiences. Trojena inks partnership agreement with Collective Retreats.
Collective Retreats, a pioneer in outdoor experiential hospitality, will open an ultra- sustainable retreat in Trojena, featuring 60 open-air guest rooms and innovative meeting space concepts. The Chedi Trojena to open in 2026.
The Chedi Trojena will offer guests a range of experiences inspired by Trojena’s six distinctive districts: Gateway, Discover, Valley, Explore, Relax, and Fun. Located in the Relax district, the hotel will offer culinary, sports, health and wellness, and restorative and meditative activities, all with stunning views of the Lake of Trojena. Trojena set to host of the 2029 Asian Winter Games.
Trojena was elected as the host city at the 41st OCA General Assembly on 4 October 2022 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. This will be the first Asian Winter Games held in Saudi Arabia, as well as in Arabian Peninsula. It will also be the first winter sporting event held in an Arabic-speaking country.
01.10.2022
Trojena set to become Saudi Arabia’s first all-year mountain resort.
Trojena is located in the Sarawat Mountains with altitudes ranging from 1500 to 2600 m altitude, 50km from the Red Sea and close to the Jordanian border. Snow falls nearly every year in Trojena but almost all snow will be manmade. By 2030, the mountain resort is expecting to attract 700,000 visitors and 7,000 permanent residents to live within the projects and adjacent residential districts. Trojena is expected to be completed and to welcome visitors and new residents in late 2026.
