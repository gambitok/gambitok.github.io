NEOM unveils Utamo, immersive destination for art and entertainment.
An innovative event space to host global artists, Utamo is the latest destination to be announced. Set within a mountain along the Gulf of Aqaba, Utamo combines natural beauty with cutting-edge technology. The theatre area encompasses a multipurpose performance space, VIP lounges, and signature restaurants, setting a global standard for events and artistic installations.
29.11.2023
NEOM announces exclusive tourism escape Siranna.
The extraordinary setting of Siranna, accessible only by boat, combines mountain, wadi and a rugged sea coast, offers 100 hotel rooms, fine dining, boutique shops and beach club.
15.11.2023
NEOM announces luxury coastal tourism – Epicon
A shimmering sanctuary rising like a mirage from Neom’s desert landscape. Nestled within this zone is the Epicon Hotel, a 55-room marvel, boasting visionary architecture and luxury experiences. A short distance away, the Epicon Resort offers tranquillity with 120 luxury rooms and 45 stunning beach villas, redefining coastal living with private pools and direct beach access.
15.10.2023
NEOM announces futuristic wellness retreat – Leyja.
Leyja builds on Neom’s commitment to ecotourism. Situated between high mountains, Leyja starts from the Gulf of Aqaba coast and winds inland to form a natural valley. Three intelligently designed hotels offer distinct experiences – an active adventure property, a gateway to exploration, and an immersive wellness retreat.
