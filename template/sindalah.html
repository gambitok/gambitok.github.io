14.11.2023
Sindalah to partner with global superyacht provider, Burgess.
Leading up to the island's 2024 opening, the partnership with leading global superyacht provider is part of NEOM's vision of Sindalah becoming a global yachting destination. Burgess will supply Sindalah's yachting clientele with a comprehensive suite of services covering all their yachting needs.
31.10.2023
Sindalah partners with Dive Butler for premium diving experience for guests.
Dive Butler will operate a five-star Professional Association of Diving Instructors dive center in Sindalah enabling a comprehensive range of marine activities, including tailored underwater excursions and exploration. Dive Butler will ensure an eco-friendly experience using electric and hybrid vessels from environmentally friendly moorings.
28.09.2023
Sindalah to cooperate with superyacht maintenance and repair company MB92 group.
MB92 Group and NEOM will establish a 10,000 square-meter repair facility for yachts at Sindalah, consolidating the island as a destination for superyachts.
24.08.2023
JLS Yachts, leading Middle East-based yacht agency and concierge service provider, to be 'key partner superyacht agency' for Sindalah.
A JLS Yachts office at the marina on the island will provide travel arrangements, customs clearance, transportation, and vital berthing, provisioning and bunkering services.
10.08.2023
IMG Golf Course Services and NEOM to open 9-hole beachfront golf course on Sindalah
The GEO certified, environmentally friendly 9-hole golf course will also include a 280-meter driving range and onsite sports club with gym, Olympic-size swimming pool, spa, and sports courts.
26.06.2023
BWA yachting to provide world-class services at Sindalah's international superyacht marina.
Global leader in superyacht concierge and maritime agency services, BWA Yachting, will establish an office to provide customs and clearance procedures, logistics, shipping, concierge and provisioning services.
08.06.2023
IGY Marinas, the global network of Luxury marinas, to develop and operate the Sindalah marina.
Sindalah is destined to become the Red Sea's ultra-prime superyacht marina closest to the Mediterranean and European seas, truly the glamorous gateway to the Red Sea Africa and Asia. The marina will offer 86 berths for yachts up to 50 m and additional serviced offshore buoys for superyachts up to 180 m and is expected to be open for business in the first half of 2024.
14.05.2023
NEOM secures SAR 3bn debt financing for development of Sindalah from Riyadh Bank
The Riyad Bank SAR 3 billion debt financing makes Sindalah the first NEOM region to be financed through bank debt, showcasing NEOM's commitment to attract diverse forms of capital and involve the private sector.
30.04.2023
Four Seasons announces plans to build island resort on Sindalah.
Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts, the world's leading luxury hospitality company, will build a luxury resort featuring 225 guest rooms and suites with terraces overlooking the beaches of the Red Sea, 52 villas with one to four-bedroom configurations with plunge pools and beach views, and with the Presidential and Royal Villas offering private swimming pools, gyms and access to private yacht docks
08.03.2023
Marriott International to open three luxury hotels on Sindalah.
Saudi Arabia's first Autograph Collection Hotels property and two Luxury Collection properties are planned. The Luxury Collection hotels will offer a stunning beach resort boasting 70 luxurious rooms and suites, including spacious villas complete with private pools. The second property will be an all-suite sanctuary featuring 115 elegant one-, two-, and three-bedroom suites. Plans for the Autograph Collection include 66 rooms and suites and villas.
